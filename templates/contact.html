FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions.

Is Budget Master free to use?

Yes! Budget Master is completely free to use. You can register and access all core features — expense tracking, budget management, loan tracking, insights, and reports — at no cost.

Does Budget Master connect to my bank account?

No. Budget Master is a manual tracking tool. You enter your transactions yourself. We never connect to your bank, credit card, or any financial institution. This means your banking credentials are never at risk.

How do I delete my account and data?

Send us an email at privacy@budgetmaster.com with your registered email address, and we'll permanently delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

Is my financial data secure?

Yes. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. All data is isolated per user account and transmitted over HTTPS. We never sell or share your financial data with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Can I export my financial data?

Yes! Navigate to the Reports page and click Download TXT next to any month to export a detailed plain-text report of your transactions and budget status.

I forgot my password. What do I do?

Go to the Forgot Password page, enter your registered email address, and follow the reset instructions sent to your inbox.
