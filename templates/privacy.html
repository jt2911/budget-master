Your data is safe

We never sell your personal or financial information to third parties.

Our Commitment to You

Budget Master ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal and financial information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our service.

01

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you register for Budget Master, we collect your name, email address, and a hashed (encrypted) version of your password. We never store your password in plain text.

Financial Data

To provide our budgeting features, we store the financial records you voluntarily enter, including:

  • Transaction amounts, dates, categories, and notes
  • Budget limits you set for each spending category
  • Loan details, interest rates, and payment history you enter

Important: Budget Master does not connect to your bank accounts. All financial data is manually entered by you.

Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information about how you interact with Budget Master, such as pages visited, features used, and session duration. This data is anonymised and used only to improve the platform.

02

How We Use Your Data

We use your information for the following purposes:

Account Management

To create and maintain your account, authenticate logins, and process password resets.

Service Delivery

To generate your financial reports, budget tracking, and AI-powered spending insights.

Communications

To send important account notifications, security alerts, and service updates (no spam).

Improvements

To analyse anonymised usage patterns and continuously improve Budget Master's features.

03

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

Password Hashing — Passwords are hashed using Werkzeug's PBKDF2 algorithm. We never store plain-text passwords.
HTTPS Encryption — All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL.
Session Security — Secure, server-side sessions are used with rotating secret keys to prevent hijacking.
Access Controls — All financial data is isolated per user. Users can only access their own records.

While we work hard to protect your data, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. Please use a strong, unique password and enable account security features.

04

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Budget Master never sells, rents, or trades your personal or financial information to third parties.

We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred with full notice to you.
  • With your consent: We may share data for other purposes with your explicit permission.
05

Cookies & Sessions

Budget Master uses session cookies to keep you logged in and provide a seamless experience. These cookies are:

  • Essential: Required for the application to function (login sessions, CSRF protection).
  • Temporary: Session cookies expire when you close your browser, or after a defined inactivity period.
  • Non-tracking: We do not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.
06

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access — Request a copy of your stored data.
Correction — Update or correct inaccurate data.
Deletion — Request permanent deletion of your account and data.
Portability — Export your data via the Reports feature.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us.

07

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you choose to delete your account, we will permanently erase all associated personal and financial data within 30 days. Anonymised, aggregated usage statistics may be retained indefinitely for platform improvement purposes.

08

Children's Privacy

Budget Master is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

09

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on our website or sending an email to your registered address. Continued use of Budget Master after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
